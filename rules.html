Rules

    1. Only students not part of the institute are allowed to participate in the Online mode.
    2. The participants should register before 9:00pm on 19th of April 2023.
    3. Participants are not allowed to make teams with members part of JIIT, but can form teams with students outside of the institute.
    4. Each team should consist of at least 3 and at most 4 members.
    5. Participants must follow the given theme or topic for the hackathon.
    6. Participants must not use any copyrighted materials.
    7. Participants must submit their final project within the given time limit.
    8. Participants must not use any illegal or unethical methods to win the competition.
    9. Any kind of misbehavior or violation of the rules can lead to disqualification from the competition.
    10. Participants must use their own equipment and internet connection for the competition.
